Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us. This policy explains what information we collect and how we use it.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect personal information that you provide to us directly, such as your name and email address when you contact us or subscribe to our newsletter. We also collect anonymous data through cookies and analytics tools to understand how our website is used.

2. How We Use Your Information

The information we collect is used to improve our website, provide you with a better user experience, and communicate with you. We do not sell or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes.

We use cookies to personalize your experience and analyze our website traffic. You can choose to disable cookies in your browser settings, but this may affect the functionality of our website.

4. Your Rights

You have the right to access, update, or delete your personal information. If you have any questions or concerns about our privacy policy, please contact us.
